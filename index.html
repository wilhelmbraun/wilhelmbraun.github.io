About

Hello and welcome! I am a researcher based at Kiel University (CAU), Germany, where I am part of the Department of Electrical and Information Engineering. I currently work on early nervous systems in several model species (cnidarians, cubozoans) and functional biologically plausible network dynamics. I also have a background in applied mathematics, particularly in the numerical solution of SDEs and PDEs applied to neuronal modeling.
